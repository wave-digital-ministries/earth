Tips for conservation and saving money!

1. Reduce your shower times.

2. Recycle your paper, metal, plastic, and glass items.

(Disposable products, soda cans, bottles, etc.)

3. Turn off not needed lights and appliances.

4. Make compost out of your plant waste.

(Coffee grounds, potatoe skins, etc. This makes excellent compost for gardens!)

5. Turn off your electronics when you're not using them.
