Ocean Care

Thus, when one portian is abused, it effects the rest.

Abuse of the ocean causes wildlife populations to decrease. Some of the biggest problems that threaten aquatic wildlife are pollution and over-fishing.

Let's take care of the oceans.

You can help us do this by:

Cleaning up your trash when you go to the beach,

Properly disposing of plastic waste,

and cleaning up addition waste from beaches (Even if it isn't yours).
